About me

Hi, I'm Zan.

I mostly do code, namely Java and Android, with a healthy dose of Ruby, Javascript and Actionscript thrown in. That and some Scala on the side for a functional seasoning. Generally I am not picky or choosy about that. I was last seen somewhere around 51 and a half degrees latitude and zero-ish longitude, aka London.

Currently I work at Marks at Spencer'sNative Shopping apps team, where we automate things that allow people to shop 24/7 from anywhere. Prior to that I was working in Staff Assisted Applications, also at M&S.

In my spare time I am learning Scala & the principles of reactive programming using Akka and RxJava libraries.

Prior to that I cofounded EqualEyes, the company behind eponymous smartphone for the blind and visually impaired. We went through angel investment, getting the first paying users and an accellerator programme here in London.

Before that I was in Slovenia, doing my masters in CS at the University of Maribor.

Besides code, I like gadgets, machines, sci-fi, skiing and reading, among other things.
